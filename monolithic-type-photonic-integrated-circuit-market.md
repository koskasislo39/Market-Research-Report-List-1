<p><p>The Monolithic Type Photonic Integrated Circuit (PIC) market is divided into two key segments: Passive and Active Photonic Integrated Circuits. Passive PICs primarily involve components that do not require external power for operation, such as waveguides and filters, focusing on signal routing and manipulation. In contrast, Active PICs include elements that actively control light, such as lasers and detectors, requiring power to enable functions like amplification and modulation. Both types are crucial for applications in telecommunications, data centers, and sensors.</p></p>
