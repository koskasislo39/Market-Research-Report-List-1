<p><strong>The Certificate Authority Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Extended Validation</li><li>Organization Validation</li><li>Domain Validation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Certificate Authorities (CAs) provide different types of SSL/TLS certificates based on validation levels: Extended Validation (EV), Organization Validation (OV), and Domain Validation (DV). EV certificates offer the highest level of trust, requiring thorough verification of the organization’s identity. OV certificates validate the organization’s identity but with less stringent checks than EV. DV certificates only confirm domain ownership through simpler methods, which makes them the quickest and least expensive option. Together, these market types address varying security needs and trust levels.</p></p>
