<p><p>The SiC coated graphite susceptor market features two primary types: pancake and barrel susceptors. Pancake susceptors are flat, circular designs that facilitate uniform heat distribution, ideal for processes requiring rapid thermal response. In contrast, barrel susceptors are cylindrical, providing a larger surface area for heating applications and enhancing temperature stability. Both types serve critical roles in semiconductor manufacturing and other industries by improving thermal efficiency and enabling precise control during high-temperature processes.</p></p>
