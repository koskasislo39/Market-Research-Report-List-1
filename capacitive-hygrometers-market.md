<p><strong>The Capacitive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive hygrometers are devices used to measure humidity by detecting changes in capacitance due to moisture absorption by hygroscopic materials. The market is divided into two types: relative and absolute. Relative type hygrometers measure humidity as a percentage of the maximum moisture the air can hold at a given temperature. Absolute type hygrometers, on the other hand, provide the actual amount of water vapor present in the air, typically expressed in grams per cubic meter, offering precise measurements for various applications.</p></p>
