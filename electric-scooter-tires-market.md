<p><ul><li>Tubeless Tires</li><li>Tubed Tires</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electric scooter tires market primarily features two types: tubeless and tubed tires. Tubeless tires offer advantages such as reduced weight, better puncture resistance, and improved handling since they do not require an inner tube. They often provide a smoother ride and are more resistant to air loss. In contrast, tubed tires are typically easier to repair, less expensive, and widely available. However, they are heavier and more prone to punctures, requiring regular maintenance to ensure optimal performance.</p></p>
