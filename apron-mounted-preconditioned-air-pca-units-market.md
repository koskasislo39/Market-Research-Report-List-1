<p><p>Apron-mounted Preconditioned Air (PCA) units are essential for providing aircraft with temperature-controlled air while on the ground. The market for these units is divided into two main types: engine-driven and electric. Engine-driven PCA units utilize the aircraft's engines to generate the necessary air flow, often providing higher output but depending on fuel consumption. Electric PCA units, on the other hand, rely on external power sources, offering quieter operation and reduced emissions, making them increasingly popular in environmentally-conscious airports.</p></p>
