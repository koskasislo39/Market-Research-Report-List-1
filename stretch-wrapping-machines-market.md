<p><p>Stretch wrapping machines are categorized into three main types based on their automation level. Fully automatic machines operate independently, efficiently wrapping products without human intervention, ideal for high-volume production. Semi-automatic machines require some manual input but automate key processes, balancing efficiency and operator involvement, suitable for medium-sized operations. Manual machines rely entirely on operator skills for wrapping, offering flexibility and lower initial costs, best for smaller operations with varied product sizes and limited volume. Each type serves different operational needs.</p></p>
