<p><p>The allergic rhinitis treatment market comprises several key types. Antihistamines alleviate symptoms like sneezing and itchy eyes by blocking histamine release. Intranasal corticosteroids reduce nasal inflammation and are often considered first-line therapy. Immunotherapies, such as allergy shots, target the underlying allergic response for long-term relief. Decongestants relieve nasal congestion by constricting blood vessels. Other treatments may include saline sprays or leukotriene receptor antagonists, catering to diverse patient needs and preferences for managing allergic rhinitis symptoms.</p></p>
