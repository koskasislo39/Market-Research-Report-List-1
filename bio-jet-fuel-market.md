<p><ul><li>Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO)</li><li>Fischer Tropsch (FT)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bio jet fuel market comprises various production methods, including Hydrogenated Vegetable Oil (HVO) and Fischer-Tropsch (FT) processes. HVO is derived from the hydrogenation of vegetable oils or animal fats, resulting in a hydrocarbon product suitable for aviation use. Conversely, the Fischer-Tropsch method converts biomass or natural gas into liquid hydrocarbons through a series of chemical reactions. Both types offer a sustainable alternative to conventional jet fuels, helping to reduce carbon emissions and enhance energy security in the aviation sector.</p></p>
