<p><p>The Perfluorosulfonic Acid Membrane market is categorized into two main types: extruded membranes and dispersed membranes. Extruded membranes are created through a continuous process that involves shaping the polymer into thin sheets, enhancing uniformity and mechanical strength. In contrast, dispersed membranes are produced by dispersing the polymer in a solution, leading to more flexibility and tunable properties. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to various applications such as fuel cells, electrolyzers, and other electrochemical processes.</p></p>
