<p><ul><li>Multi-Axis</li><li>Single-Axis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>MEMS optical accelerometers are categorized into two primary market types: multi-axis and single-axis accelerometers. Single-axis accelerometers measure acceleration along one direction, making them suitable for applications requiring simple tilt detection or monitoring. In contrast, multi-axis accelerometers can measure acceleration across multiple directions simultaneously, providing comprehensive motion data. This versatility makes multi-axis types ideal for complex applications such as robotics, automotive systems, and consumer electronics, where nuanced movement tracking is essential for enhanced performance and functionality.</p></p>
