<p><strong>The Transmission Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reciprocating</li><li>Rotary</li><li>Centrifugal</li><li>Axial Flow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transmission compressor market comprises various types, each suited to specific applications. Reciprocating compressors utilize pistons to compress gas, offering high pressure and versatility. Rotary compressors, including rotary screw and vane types, provide continuous airflow and are efficient for moderate pressures. Centrifugal compressors use high-speed rotating impellers, making them ideal for large volume applications with lower pressure ratios. Axial flow compressors move air or gas parallel to the axis, delivering high flow rates and efficiency, commonly used in jet engines and heavy industry.</p></p>
