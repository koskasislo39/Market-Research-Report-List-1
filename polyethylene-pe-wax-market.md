<p><p>The Polyethylene (PE) Wax market encompasses three key types: Polymerized PE Wax, Modified PE Wax, and Cracked PE Wax. Polymerized PE Wax is produced through polymerization processes, offering high purity and unique performance characteristics. Modified PE Wax, often enhanced with additives, serves diverse applications in coatings and inks, improving properties like adhesion and durability. Cracked PE Wax, derived from thermal or catalytic cracking of polyethylene, provides a cost-effective option with varied viscosities, widely used in industrial applications and formulations.</p></p>
