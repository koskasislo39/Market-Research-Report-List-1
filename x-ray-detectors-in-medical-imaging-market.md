<p><p>X-ray detectors play a crucial role in medical imaging, primarily categorized into dynamic and static imaging types. Dynamic imaging detectors capture real-time moving images, allowing for detailed assessment of physiological functions, such as cardiac motion or gastrointestinal dynamics. In contrast, static imaging detectors are designed for high-resolution, still images, ideal for diagnosing conditions like fractures or tumors. Both types enhance diagnostic accuracy and patient care, but serve different clinical purposes based on the imaging requirements.</p></p>
