<p><p>The rail heaters market comprises three main types: electric heating, gas heating, and steam heating. Electric heating utilizes electrical energy to warm rail tracks, promoting optimal performance in cold conditions. Gas heating employs combustion of gas to generate heat, offering an efficient and quick solution for track maintenance. Steam heating utilizes steam generated from water to raise the temperature of rail components, preventing ice formation and ensuring safe train operations. Each type serves to enhance railway safety and efficiency.</p></p>
