<p><p>The wax additives market comprises three primary types: natural waxes, semi-synthetic waxes, and synthetic waxes. Natural waxes are derived from plant or animal sources, offering biodegradable and eco-friendly properties. Semi-synthetic waxes are modified natural waxes, enhancing performance characteristics while maintaining some natural benefits. Synthetic waxes, on the other hand, are completely man-made, providing consistent quality and versatile applications across industries like coatings, plastics, and cosmetics, driven by specific performance and thermal properties. Each type caters to distinct market needs.</p></p>
