<p><strong>The Tabletop (Desktop, Benchtop) SEM Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>W-SEM</li><li>FEG-SEM</li><li>FIB-SEM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tabletop SEM market encompasses different types of scanning electron microscopes, primarily categorized into W-SEM, FEG-SEM, and FIB-SEM. W-SEM, or tungsten filament SEM, offers basic imaging capabilities and is cost-effective. FEG-SEM, utilizing field emission guns, provides higher resolution and enhanced imaging quality for advanced applications. FIB-SEM combines a focused ion beam with SEM for sample preparation and analysis, enabling detailed 3D imaging and micro-manipulation. These systems cater to various industries including materials science, semiconductor, and biological research.</p></p>
