<p><p>The semiconductor butterfly valve market features primarily two types of stainless steel materials: SS304 and SS316L. SS304, known for its good corrosion resistance and high-temperature capabilities, is commonly used in less aggressive environments. In contrast, SS316L offers enhanced resistance to chloride-induced corrosion and is preferred for more demanding applications in the semiconductor industry. These materials ensure durability and reliability in controlling fluid flow, playing a crucial role in semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
