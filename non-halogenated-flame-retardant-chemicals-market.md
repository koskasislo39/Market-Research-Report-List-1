Boron compounds enhance thermal stability and form protective layers during heating. Phosphorus-based retardants create char barriers, while nitrogen compounds reduce flammability by releasing non-combustible gases. Other materials include organic additives and coatings that provide fire resistance while being environmentally friendly, together catering to diverse applications across industries.</p></p>
