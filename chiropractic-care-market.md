<p><p>Chiropractic care can be categorized into three primary market types: maintenance care, corrective care, and relief care. Maintenance care focuses on long-term health by preventing future issues through regular adjustments. Corrective care aims to address specific musculoskeletal problems and restore proper function, often requiring more intensive treatment. Relief care is geared towards immediate symptom alleviation, helping patients manage pain or discomfort quickly. Each type serves distinct needs, catering to various patient preferences and health goals.</p></p>
