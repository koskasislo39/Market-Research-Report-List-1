<p><p>The feed acidifier market is categorized into three main types: compound acidifiers, organic acidifiers, and inorganic acidifiers. Compound acidifiers are blends of different acids designed to enhance feed stability and palatability. Organic acidifiers, derived from natural sources, promote gut health and improve nutrient absorption while exhibiting antimicrobial properties. Inorganic acidifiers, typically mineral-based, are primarily used to lower pH levels in feed, enhancing preservation and assisting digestion. Each type serves distinct functions to optimize animal health and feed efficiency.</p></p>
