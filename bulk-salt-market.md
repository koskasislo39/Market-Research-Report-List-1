<p><p>The bulk salt market comprises three main types: rock salt, solar salt, and evaporated salt. Rock salt is mined from underground deposits and is often used for de-icing roads and in industrial processes. Solar salt is harvested through evaporating seawater in large ponds, primarily used for cooking and food preservation. Evaporated salt is produced through the evaporation of brine, resulting in pure salt commonly used in food processing and chemical industries, catering to diverse applications and consumer needs.</p></p>
