<p><p>The Switching PTC Thermistors market is categorized into two main types: Self Heating Mode and Sensor Mode. In Self Heating Mode, thermistors generate heat when a current flows through them, providing overcurrent protection by switching off the circuit in response to temperature increase. Conversely, in Sensor Mode, these thermistors monitor temperature changes within a system, providing precise temperature readings that can trigger actions in applications like automotive and consumer electronics. Both modes enhance safety and reliability in various electronic applications.</p></p>
