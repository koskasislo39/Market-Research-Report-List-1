<p><p>The detox fiber drink market is primarily divided into two types: soluble fiber drinks and insoluble fiber drinks. Soluble fiber drinks dissolve in water, helping to regulate blood sugar levels, lower cholesterol, and enhance digestion by forming a gel-like substance. In contrast, insoluble fiber drinks do not dissolve but add bulk to the digestive system, promoting regular bowel movements and preventing constipation. Both types cater to health-conscious consumers seeking digestive support and detoxification benefits.</p></p>
