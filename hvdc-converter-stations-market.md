<p><p>HVDC converter stations facilitate the conversion of direct current (DC) to alternating current (AC) and vice versa, playing a crucial role in electricity transmission. The market types include monopolar systems, using a single conductor; bipolar systems, employing two conductors for balanced transmission; back-to-back systems, connecting two AC grids directly; and multi-terminal systems, allowing several connections for complex networks. Each type serves specific needs in efficiency, stability, and grid interconnection, influencing market dynamics and technology development in the HVDC sector.</p></p>
