<p><p>The acute ischemic stroke therapeutics market comprises several key types. Thrombolytic therapeutics, such as tissue plasminogen activator (tPA), aim to dissolve blood clots quickly. Antihypertensive therapeutics help manage blood pressure to prevent further complications. Antiplatelet therapeutics, like aspirin, reduce platelet aggregation to prevent new clot formation. Anticoagulants, such as warfarin and direct oral anticoagulants, inhibit blood coagulation processes. Together, these therapies play a crucial role in improving outcomes for patients experiencing acute ischemic strokes.</p></p>
