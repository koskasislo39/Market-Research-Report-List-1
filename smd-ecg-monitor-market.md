<p><p>The SMD ECG monitor market encompasses various types, including Holter ECG testers and static ECG testers. Holter ECG testers are portable devices designed for continuous monitoring of a patient's heart activity over 24 to 48 hours, providing insights into heart rhythm abnormalities during daily activities. In contrast, static ECG testers capture and analyze heart activity at a single point in time, typically in a clinical setting. Both devices are essential for diagnosing and managing cardiovascular conditions effectively, catering to different monitoring needs.</p></p>
