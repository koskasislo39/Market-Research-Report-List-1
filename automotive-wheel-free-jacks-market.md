<p><p>The Automotive Wheel Free Jacks market comprises two primary types: Manual and Air-Hydraulic. In contrast, Air-Hydraulic jacks employ compressed air to enhance lifting efficiency and speed, allowing for heavier loads with less physical effort. Both types cater to different user preferences and requirements in automotive maintenance and repair, supporting diverse operational environments in workshops and garages.</p></p>
