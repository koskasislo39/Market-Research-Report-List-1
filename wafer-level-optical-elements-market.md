<p><p>Wafer Level Optical Elements (WLOEs) encompass various types designed for precision and compactness in optical applications. Micro-Lens Arrays consist of numerous tiny lenses for enhanced light manipulation and imaging. Shack-Hartmann Lens Arrays are utilized for wavefront sensing, improving optical system performance. Uniform Compound Eye Lenses mimic nature's compound eyes, offering wide-field imaging with minimal distortion. Laser Collimators focus light into a parallel beam, optimizing performance in laser systems. Together, these elements revolutionize efficiency and miniaturization in optical technologies.</p></p>
