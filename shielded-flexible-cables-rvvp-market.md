<p><p>Shielded Flexible Cables, commonly known as RVVP, are specialized cables designed to provide excellent electrical performance while ensuring protection against electromagnetic interference (EMI) and physical damage. These cables typically consist of multiple strands of copper wire, surrounded by an insulating layer and a shield, which can be made from metallic or non-metallic materials. RVVP cables are widely used in various applications, including automation systems, machine tools, telecommunications, and power distribution due to their reliability and durability.</p><p>The Shielded Flexible Cables (RVVP) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.7% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand across several industries, such as manufacturing, automotive, and telecommunications.
