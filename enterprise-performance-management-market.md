<p><p>Enterprise Performance Management (EPM) encompasses various market types, notably on-premises and cloud-based solutions. On-premises EPM involves software installed locally on a company's servers, offering control and customization but requiring significant maintenance and upfront investment. In contrast, cloud-based EPM solutions are hosted on external servers, providing scalability, accessibility, and reduced IT overhead, with subscription-based pricing. Businesses often choose based on factors like budget, IT resources, and the need for real-time data access and collaboration across teams.</p></p>
