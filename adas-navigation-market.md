<p><p>ADAS navigation market encompasses various sensor types, each serving unique functions. Image sensors capture visual data for lane detection and obstacle recognition. Radar sensors use radio waves to gauge distance and speed of nearby objects, enhancing adaptive cruise control. LiDAR employs laser beams to create high-resolution 3D maps of surroundings. Ultrasonic sensors measure proximity for parking assistance, while infrared sensors detect heat signatures, useful for pedestrian alerts. Other technologies may include GPS and GNSS for precise navigation solutions.</p></p>
